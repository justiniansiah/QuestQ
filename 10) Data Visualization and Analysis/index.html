Data Visualisations and Analysis

In this section, we visualize the examine the data collected over several peak periods in a calendar week. We assume that that the distibution of peak periods doesnt differ between different days. (ie., Thursday lunch peak period and Wednesday lunch peak period are the same.)

Below, we visualize lead time, interarrival time, and queue length (# customers waiting in queue) statistics.

Peak Lead Time

 

In the figure on the left we observe the change of lead time over the total number of customers during various peak hour periods. Each period can be differentiated by the second series which denotes the total number of customers in each period.
